Pidam University

Message by Rector

PIDAM University is created and exist to provide quality education to young generations of Puntland and Somalia as a whole, so as to give them valuable opportunities to apply the latest technologies and knowledge and at large to take Puntland in the midst of the competitive societies. The University gained recognition from our community and government as well. As being an accredited University, we will maintain quality reputation of our diploma. In addition to the accreditation, we have solid evidence that supports our mission of "quality education." For example, during the 1990 to 2012 our students who finished their academic programs achieved superior positions in both public and private sector organizations as well as graduate degree in local universities. Others continue their Post Graduate studies in internationally recognized universities in Britain, Australia, United States of America, China, Malaysia, Egypt, Sudan, Uganda, Kenya, Ethiopia and Yemen.
Prof Mohamed Mumin Yusuf
Rector of University.
