How we are preparing students for the jobs

  • We are preparing our students for jobs in various ways, namely practical training for course taken in the Univeristy. Our approach for training is most practical, in each course real world problems are simulated to solve as an exercise. Each and every theme in the courses are dealt or solved with exercise and problems related to practical business management problems.
    There are continuous assessment tests in every two weeks, and also students are assigned to prepare mini-projects in individuals and in groups and make presentations in front of the classes. Class attendances are recorded and closely controlled.
    Mid-term and final examinations rationally reflect the content of the courses taken and question papers are strictly are marked to ensure that those who do not study will not pass.

  • When dealing with practical aspects of course themes, examples and exercise relevant to puntland and Somali situation are presented and solved in order enhance student understanding and familiarity.

  • Students are sent to work for internship to business companies in Bosaso so as to gain valuable practical job experience and to expose them to practical work situations.

  • Business companies are approached and requested to advertise vacant jobs and take competitive exams so the learned and best students will acquire the jobs.
